Help Center

Answers to frequently asked questions

🔍
Account
How do I create an account? ▾
Tap "Sign Up" on the login screen. You can register with Google (recommended) or use an email and password. Verification email will be sent if you use email sign-up.
Can I change my username? ▾
Yes! Go to Settings → Profile → Edit Username. You can change it once every 30 days. Usernames must be 3–20 characters.
I forgot my password. What do I do? ▾
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
Gameplay
How does the rating system work? ▾
We use an ELO-based rating system. Win a match and gain up to 25 points; lose and drop up to 20. Rating changes scale based on your opponent's rating — beating a higher-rated opponent earns more points.
What happens if the connection drops mid-game? ▾
You have 30 seconds to reconnect. If you don't reconnect in time, the match is forfeited. Rated matches will count as a loss if forfeit. Casual matches are cancelled.
How does the timer work? ▾
Each player has 30 seconds per turn by default. In ranked play, time runs out = auto-forfeit. You can adjust turn time in custom (private) matches.
Tokens & Shop
How do I earn tokens? ▾
Tokens are earned by winning matches, completing daily quests, logging in daily (streak bonus), and reaching achievements. You can also purchase token packs in the Shop.
Are purchases refundable? ▾
Token purchases are non-refundable once spent. If you purchased tokens and did not receive them, contact support within 48 hours with your transaction ID.
Can I transfer tokens to other players? ▾
Token transfers are not currently supported to prevent abuse. This feature may be considered in a future update.
Technical
Which browsers are supported? ▾
TicTacArena works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). For the best experience, we recommend Chrome on desktop or our mobile app.
How do I report a bug? ▾
Use the "Report" button in-game or visit our Contact page. Include your device, browser version, and steps to reproduce the bug. Screenshots are always helpful!
