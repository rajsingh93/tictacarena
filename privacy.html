1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, profile photo
  • Game Data: Match history, ratings, achievements, in-game actions
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Payment Info: Transaction IDs only (payment details handled by Stripe)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Operate and improve the TicTacArena platform
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Send important service notifications
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and ensure fair play
  • Generate anonymized analytics to improve game design

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics tools
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or court order
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

All third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how users navigate the platform

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data portability (export your data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@tictacarena.com

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and access controls. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

TicTacArena is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately for removal.
